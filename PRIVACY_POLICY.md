# SlideNotes Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

SlideNotes is a browser extension that helps users capture screenshots and export them as a PDF.

## Data Collection and Use

- SlideNotes does not collect, sell, or share personal data with external servers.
- SlideNotes does not include analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising SDKs.
- SlideNotes does not require account creation or login.

## Local Storage

- Captured screenshots are stored locally in the browser using `chrome.storage.local`.
- PDF generation is performed locally on the user's device.
- Users can delete all saved screenshots at any time using the extension's Clear All feature.

## Permissions

SlideNotes may request browser permissions such as active tab access, tab capture, local storage, downloads, and context menus. These permissions are used only to provide core extension features.

## Third-Party Services

SlideNotes does not send captured content to third-party services.

## Children's Privacy

SlideNotes is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
